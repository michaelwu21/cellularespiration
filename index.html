What is cellular respiration?

Cellular Respiration is a series of chemical reactions that convert the energy in food molecules into ATP
ATP is a form of energy that your body uses to really, do anything, from jumping around screaming to performing active transport(A form of transporting molecules in/out of the cell using energy)
